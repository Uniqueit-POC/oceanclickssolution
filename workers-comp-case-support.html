Medical Records, Treatment Tracking & Chronologies

Medical documentation is central to every workers' compensation case. Treatment timelines, injury progression, and diagnosis clarity directly impact benefit determinations and settlement value. These structured summaries help attorneys demonstrate injury severity, disability impact, return-to-work limitations, and claim legitimacy with clarity and precision.

Medical record requests from treating physicians and facilities

Sorting and indexing of medical charts, bills, and reports

Preparation of medical chronologies summarizing treatment progression

Tracking of follow-up appointments, referrals, and IMEs

Monitoring of work restrictions and physician evaluations

Treatment timeline creation for case evaluation and settlement

Evidence, Statements & Workplace Documentation Support

Workers' compensation cases often require multiple forms of evidence to support injury claims. We ensure every file contains the organized documentation attorneys need for negotiation, benefits advocacy, or litigation.

Incident report summaries

Employer and coworker statement organization

Photographic and video evidence review

Surveillance summary assistance for contested claims

Job duty and workplace environment documentation

Hazard-related notes and analysis (as provided by the client)
