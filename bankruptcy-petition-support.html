Chapter 7 & Chapter 13 Case Preparation

Each bankruptcy chapter has unique documentation demands. We support attorneys with accurate, timely drafting across both major filing types.

Chapter 7

Liquidation Cases

  • Eligibility evaluation support through median income analysis
  • Completion of Form 122A means test
  • Preparation of Schedules A–J
  • Identification and documentation of exempt vs. non-exempt assets
  • Compilation of unsecured debt lists
  • Organization of supporting financial documentation
  • Drafting of SOFA and petition components under attorney supervision

Chapter 13

Wage Earner Plans

  • Disposable income determination (Form 122C)
  • Budget analysis and repayment feasibility
  • Schedule drafting with long-term restructuring considerations
  • Assistance with preliminary Chapter 13 plan inputs
  • Creditor categorization (priority, secured, unsecured)
  • Income verification and expense analysis

By outsourcing Chapter 7 and Chapter 13 petition preparation, firms can improve efficiency, reduce backlog, and ensure compliance with U.S. Trustee guidelines.
