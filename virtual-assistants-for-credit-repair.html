What Is a Credit Repair Virtual Assistant?

A credit repair virtual assistant (CR VA) is a trained administrative professional who manages the back-office tasks of a credit repair or legal practice. Unlike generic virtual assistants, a credit repair VA understands:

  • Bureau dispute cycles
  • FCRA/FDCPA basics
  • CRM platforms like GHL, DisputeFox, Trello, mycase and CDM
  • Monthly dispute rounds
  • Credit report handling
  • Client Initial Credit Consultation & onboarding
  • Documentation and communication workflows

Your VA becomes an extension of your internal operations — without the cost, hiring complexity, or training burden of a traditional employee.
