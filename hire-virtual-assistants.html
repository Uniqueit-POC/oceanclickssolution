What Is a Virtual Assistant?

A Virtual Assistant (VA) is a remote professional who helps businesses manage administrative, operational, customer-facing, and back-office tasks. Instead of hiring an in-house employee, companies across the US increasingly hire virtual assistants to reduce costs, improve productivity, and access talent trained in modern business tools.

Today's VAs are not just administrative helpers. They are skilled professionals trained to support your business growth.
