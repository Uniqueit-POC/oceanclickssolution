Comprehensive Personal Injury Paralegal Services

We support PI attorneys, litigation teams, and case managers with end-to-end paralegal assistance across the full lifecycle of a personal injury case.

End-to-End PI Case Support Includes:

Case Intake & Initial Fact Development

Preliminary intake, client fact gathering, incident summaries, and structuring claim information for attorney review.

Medical Records Retrieval, Review & Chronologies

Request medical records, organize them, and prepare clear chronology summaries and treatment timelines—critical for damages evaluation.

Demand Letter Drafting & Settlement Package Preparation

Structured, attorney-supervised demand letters including medical expenses, lost wages, pain and suffering summaries, and supporting documentation.

Evidence Review & Documentation Support

Police reports, photographs, witness statements, videos, and property damage documentation reviewed and prepared for settlement or litigation.

Insurance Communication Assistance

Track insurer communication, follow up on outstanding requests, and maintain up-to-date claim notes under attorney direction.

Litigation Support for PI Cases

Assist with drafting complaints, motions, disclosures, discovery requests, and preparing organized exhibits for attorney review.

Each support function directly addresses the documentation-heavy nature of PI practice—creating cleaner files, faster case movement, and stronger negotiating leverage.
