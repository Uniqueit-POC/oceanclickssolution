Tenant screening is one of the most time-consuming functions inside any PM company. Every application requires multiple checks, compliance adherence, documentation handling, and follow-ups — all with quick turnaround expectations.

Slow processing leads to lost applicants and extended vacancy periods.

Rushed or inconsistent screening can result in problem tenants and delinquency.
